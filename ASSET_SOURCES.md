# Legal Asset Sources

Use only assets you can redistribute in the mod jar.

Recommended sources:

- Freesound with CC0 filter: https://freesound.org/help/faq/
- Pixabay Sound Effects: https://pixabay.com/service/faq/
- OpenGameArt, but check each asset license: https://opengameart.org/
- Sonniss GDC bundles under their bundle license: https://sonniss.com/gdc-bundle-license/
- Self-recorded sounds processed in Audacity.
- Self-generated textures/synth drones with a tool that grants commercial redistribution rights.

Do not use ripped sounds/textures from games, films, SCP projects, ARGs, YouTube uploads without a clear license, or other mods unless the mod license explicitly permits redistribution.
